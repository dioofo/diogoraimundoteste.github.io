BIOGRAPHY

I'm a junior web designer with a strong passion for creating visually compelling and user-centered digital experiences. I studied Graphic Design and Multimedia at ESAD.CR – Escola Superior de Artes e Design de Caldas da Rainha, in Portugal, where I developed a solid foundation in design thinking, UI/UX principles and front-end development.

Throughout my academic journey, I immersed myself in both conceptual and technical aspects of web design. My work is guided by attention to detail, accessibility, and clean design, always aiming to deliver intuitive and engaging user interfaces. Currently, I'm focused on growing my skills through collaborative projects, freelance work, and continuous learning.
