WEBSITE CONCEPT

This project is a complete redesign of the official website for the Swedish metalcore band Imminence. The goal is to move away from their current, generic layout and create a visually immersive and emotionally engaging digital space that truly reflects the band's unique identity. The redesign focuses on enhancing personality, interactivity, and storytelling, while maintaining a smooth user experience and seamless e-commerce integration.
