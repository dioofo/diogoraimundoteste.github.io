PROJECT OVERVIEW

NeoBuild Website Concept

NeoBuild is a conceptual architectural studio envisioned as a forward-thinking brand focused on minimalism, precision, and contemporary design. This homepage concept was developed as an exercise in clean aesthetics and functional layout, with a strong emphasis on grids, whitespace, and visual clarity.

The objective was to design a homepage that reflects the ethos of modern architecture — structured, elegant, and restrained. The result is a minimalist interface that allows form and content to breathe, showcasing the studio's work and philosophy with quiet confidence.
