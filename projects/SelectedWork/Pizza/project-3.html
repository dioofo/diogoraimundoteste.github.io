Bella Pizza Website Concept

Bella Pizza is a conceptual brand for an artisanal pizzeria that blends traditional Italian soul with a modern, design-forward online presence. This homepage concept explores how warmth, flavor, and personality can be translated into a digital experience.

The goal was to create a welcoming, visually delicious homepage that feels handcrafted yet modern — capturing the essence of a real pizza lover's haven.
